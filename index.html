About the Author
Charles Brockmeyer

Charles Brockmeyer reports that many years ago he had flopped down on a Swiss hotel bed exhausted after a couple of days without sleep. He had just flown back from a jobsite in Saudi Arabia where a tough, but successful project was completed. As he laid there in beautiful Zurich, it was though he had landed in a fairyland; clean, organized, full of beautiful people and quaint villages. All this was a in stark contrast to the land which he had just come, where it looked like the world’s largest, hottest gravel pit. He looked up from his bed and gratefully thanked God for all the amazing people, helps and miracles that had happened with the team in making this project a winner. Charles asked, “Lord, thank you for everything. You are my success, protection and help… but what can I do for You?” An internal reply came, “Tell people what God has done for you and what you know about Me.” Then He added, “But know there will be persecution.” These words have such effected me that they have become the root of my stories and the passion of my life to share them.

About “Remember All God Has Done For You”

Remember All God Has Done for You" is a heartwarming collection of true stories that showcase the presence of divine intervention in everyday life. Through tales of miracles, faith, answered prayers, and humorous moments, Charles Brockmeyer illustrates God's love and kindness in various aspects of life. The book emphasizes the importance of believing in God's love and finding hope and strength through Him. With a personal and engaging narrative, the stories in this book aim to inspire readers to reflect on their own experiences and recognize the ways in which God has touched their lives.

What keeps you Turn the Pages?

The engaging storytelling style, relatable characters, and powerful messages of faith and hope are what keep readers turning the pages of “Remember All God Has Done for You.” The genuine emotions and experiences shared in each story resonate with readers on a deep level, drawing them in and inspiring them to reflect on their own spiritual journey. The book's uplifting and heartwarming content, combined with the underlying theme of God’s presence in everyday life, creates a compelling narrative that captivates readers and leaves them eager to discover more about the miracles and blessings that can be found in the most unexpected places.
